Overview

Mission Statement

Our primary purpose is to create stylish, durable, and affordable leather products that meet the needs of modern consumers while promoting ethical and sustainable practices in leatherwork.

Vision

Katchyway seeks to become a leading brand in the leather industry, known for our craftsmanship, innovation, and commitment to sustainability.

Audience

Who are the target customers?
Our customers will range from teenage to adult fashion enthusiasts, Professionals and Business Executives, Artisans and Craftsmanship Aficionados and people who enjoy luxury and gift buying, with a focus on individuals aged 18 to 45, who have moderate to above-average disposable income, as they will be spending on durable, high quality leather products.

How will my audience usually access my site?
Our audience will access our site through their mobile devices, tablets, and desktops. They will also reach our site through social media platforms such as Facebook, linkedin, and Twitter.

What do they want?
They want leather goods that will last for years, aging gracefully over time. our customers value the durability of our products, knowing that each piece is crafted to withstand daily use while retaining its beauty. Many of our clients desire something unique that sets them apart. Whether it's a custom engraving or a bespoke design, they want leather goods that are personalized to reflect their individual tastes and preferences.
